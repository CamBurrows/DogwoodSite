Packing

Experts in Packing and Prepartion

Moving can be a very stressful experience. People often experience a time crunch trying to balance their work life with attempting to prepare for their upcoming move. This leads to many people attempting to pack everything the day before they’re supposed to move out—which is never a good idea. Here at Dogwood Moving, we’ll go the extra mile to help make your move as convenient and as stress-free as possible, which is why we offer additional packing services.

Need Boxes or Supplies For your Move?

Having the right supplies makes a big difference when you’re preparing to move. If you would like us to supply packing materials for you then there will be a one time $50 service fee plus the cost for materials used.

We still offer the packing service if you choose to purchase your own packing materials instead of having us supply the materials. The rate for our packing service is the same hourly rates as moving (For more details on our rates go to our pricing page).

Call today and ask about our packing/moving rates and pricing for supplies!

Self-booking quick tips and info:

  • 26 Foot Moving Truck + 2 Professional Movers = $110/hr
  • Add an additional mover for $35/HR
  • A crew of 4 Movers and 2-26ft box trucks is available. Please call or email to schedule.
  • As a rule of thumb when self-estimating, our trucks hold approximately 1500 sq ft of normally furnished living space and one full load typically takes 4-6 hours plus drive time.
  • Two hour minimum on all jobs.
  • There are no travels fees within Wake County.
  • Travel fees are determined by the following equation: $1 x per mile (round trip) for the address outside of Wake County.
  • Payment: We don't require any deposit or payments up front. We accept ALL payments at the completion of the move. We accept Cash, Checks, and all major credit cards (there is a 3% processing fee associated with all CC transactions).
