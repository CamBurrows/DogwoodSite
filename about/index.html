About

Meet the Owner

My name is Ryan King, and I am the Owner/Managing Partner of Dogwood Moving. We are a locally owned Residential and Commercial moving company based out of Raleigh, NC. I was raised in Wilmington, NC until the age of 18 when I attended Queens University of Charlotte as a collegiate athlete. In that time, I began working for Hornet Moving and soon becoming the General Manager. After 2 years, we ranked as one of the highest rated moving companies in North Carolina! The growing success and reputation led me to start Dogwood Moving.

The Dogwood Promise

Here at Dogwood Moving, we promise to provide excellent customer service by getting the job done safely and efficiently. I have had a unique and rewarding perspective, working my way from mover to manager to owner. This has allowed me the opportunity to connect with our customers and staff alike and see the crucial connection between employee and customer. The happier your employees, the harder they will work, which will lead to a happier customer.

This belief is one I instill in the entire Dogwood Moving team to assure that we provide a high-quality service to our customers, while continuing to grow as a successful company. This company is dedicated to quality, hard work, and excellent customer service. These three pillars are the foundation of our company, and we prove that with each and every move we complete.

Thank you,

Self-booking quick tips and info:

  • 26 Foot Moving Truck + 2 Professional Movers = $110/hr
  • Add an additional mover for $35/HR
  • A crew of 4 Movers and 2-26ft box trucks is available. Please call or email to schedule.
  • As a rule of thumb when self-estimating, our trucks hold approximately 1500 sq ft of normally furnished living space and one full load typically takes 4-6 hours plus drive time.
  • Two hour minimum on all jobs.
  • There are no travels fees within Wake County.
  • Travel fees are determined by the following equation: $1 x per mile (round trip) for the address outside of Wake County.
  • Payment: We don't require any deposit or payments up front. We accept ALL payments at the completion of the move. We accept Cash, Checks, and all major credit cards (there is a 3% processing fee associated with all CC transactions).
